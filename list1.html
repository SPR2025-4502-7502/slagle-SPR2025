American Idol

Image Credit

American Idol is a popular singing competition where aspiring artists audition and compete for a chance to win cash and a recording contract. American Idol is produced by Fremantle and 19 Entertainment, a part of Sony Pictures Television.

The Bachelorette

Image Credit

The Bachlorette is a reality dating series that follows one woman on her journey to find love among a group of elgible men. Through a series of dates, challenges, and rose ceremonies, she must decide who will be her future husband. The Bachlorette is Produced by Next Entertainment and Warner Bros. Unscripted Television.

Big Brother

Image Credit

Big Brother is a reality competition series where a group of houseguests live together in a custom-built house, completely cut from the outside world. They compete in challenges, form alliances, and vote each other out until one winner claims the grand prize. Big Brother is produced by Next Entertainment and Warner Bros. Unscripted Television.

Shark Tank

Image Credit

Shark Tank is a reality TV show where entreprenuers pitch their bussiness ideas to a panel of wealthy investors, known as "Sharks". Shark Tank is produced by MGM Television.

Love Island USA

Image Credit

Love Island is a reality dating show where singles live in a luxurious villa and couple up in hopes of finding love and winning a cash prize. Love Island is produced by ITV Entertainment.

Dance Moms

Image Credit

Dance Moms follows young competitive dancers and their mothers as they train under dance coach Abby Lee Miller. Dance Moms is produced by Collins Avenue Entertainment.

Survivor

Image Credit

Survivor is a reality competiiton where contestants are stranded in a remote location and out outplay and outlast each other to win a million dollars. Survivor is produced by Mark Burnett Productions.
